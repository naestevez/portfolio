about

Hi! I'm Alex. Born and raised in Miami. I have a thing for untying knots, solving sudoku puzzles and philosophical conversations. Along with my unquenchable thirst for new music, I have a huge appetite for studying life and learning new skills. In my early years after college, my communications and marketing skills helped me to understand people and culture. Later on, after visiting several national parks in the Pacific Northwest, I became inspired by forests and how technology was being used to monitor and protect them. Three years of independent study, a coding bootcamp experience, and one and a half years in a forestry masters program brought me to realize that I love GIS and programming and I would to create custom GIS applications. More can be found on my GIS portfolio. Otherwise, feel free to look at my other coding projects here as well.
